Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Atomic Canvas ("the App") is developed by KindKoda ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This App is designed to be suitable for all ages, including children under 13. When the App is configured for child-directed treatment:

  • Personalized advertising is disabled
  • Ad content is restricted to age-appropriate material (rated G)
  • No personal information from children is knowingly collected

4. Advertising

The App displays banner advertisements and offers optional rewarded video ads. These ads are provided by Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Data Storage

All game data (crystal balance, upgrade levels, streak progress, settings) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted to external servers.

6. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to display advertisements
  • Network State: Required to check connectivity for ad loading

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
